Everything stays in your browser's localStorage on your device. Ambit™ Budget Pro never uploads your data anywhere — no servers, no cloud, no tracking. Your financial information is 100% private.
Each install holds its own data — by design. Because Ambit™ doesn't have user accounts or cloud sync, your laptop's data and your phone's data live separately. To copy data between devices, the easiest path is Send to Device in the sidebar — Ambit hands your data file to your OS's share sheet so you can pick AirDrop, Nearby Share, email, iMessage, or save-to-Drive. The other device opens the file and imports. (If your browser doesn't support the share sheet, use Export Backup → transfer the JSON manually → Import Backup on the other device.) Either way, it's a deliberate trade-off: a one-time manual step when you set up a second device, in exchange for never handing your financial data to someone else's server.
