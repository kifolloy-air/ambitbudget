FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How is my data stored? ▼
All your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage. Nothing is ever sent to a server. You can also export your data as a JSON file for backup at any time.
Does Ambit connect to my bank? ▼
No. Ambit never asks for your bank credentials. You import transactions by downloading a CSV file from your bank's website and uploading it to Ambit. This is the safest approach — your bank login is never shared with anyone.
What banks are supported? ▼
Any bank that lets you export transactions as CSV, TSV, or Excel format — which is virtually all of them. Ambit's smart column mapper handles different formats automatically.
Can I use it on my phone? ▼
Yes! Ambit is fully responsive and works great on phones and tablets. The interface adapts with a bottom tab bar for easy navigation on smaller screens.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes — our live demo lets you explore the full app with sample data, completely free, no sign-up required. When you're ready, purchase to use it with your own data.
What's the difference between plans? ▼
All plans include every feature. The only difference is the number of bank accounts you can track: Starter supports 1 account, Plus supports 3, and Pro gives you unlimited accounts. You can upgrade anytime by purchasing a higher tier.
How does the license key work? ▼
After purchase, you receive a license key via email along with a download link. Open the downloaded file in your browser and enter your key once to activate. After that, the app works entirely offline — no internet needed.
What happens if I clear my browser data? ▼
Your data would be lost — which is why we built a one-click export feature. We recommend exporting a backup regularly. You can import it back instantly if needed.
Do I get updates? ▼
Yes. All future updates are included with your one-time purchase. New features, improvements, and fixes — all free, forever.
