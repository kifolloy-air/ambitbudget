Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

The Short Version

Ambit Budget Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your financial data never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or have access to your budgets, transactions, or account information.

Data Storage

All your financial data — accounts, transactions, budgets, categories, and payee rules — is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your computer
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Is never backed up by us (we recommend using the built-in Export feature)
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser data

License Activation

When you activate your license key, the app makes a single API call to our server, which forwards the request to LemonSqueezy's license validation API. This call transmits only:

  • Your license key
  • An instance identifier (a random string, not tied to your identity)

No personal information, financial data, or device identifiers are sent during activation. After activation, the app works entirely offline.

Payment Processing

All purchases are processed by LemonSqueezy, our payment provider. When you make a purchase, LemonSqueezy collects your email address and payment information. We do not have access to your full payment details. Please refer to LemonSqueezy's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

Cookies & Tracking

The Ambit Budget Pro application uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking scripts. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar service. The marketing website (ambitbudget.com) does not set any cookies.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party services involved are:

  • LemonSqueezy — Payment processing and license key delivery
  • Vercel — Website and API hosting
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery for the marketing website

None of these services have access to your financial data within the app.

Data We Don't Collect

To be explicit, we do not collect:

  • Bank account information or credentials
  • Transaction data or spending history
  • Budget amounts or financial goals
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device fingerprints or IP addresses

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since the app runs locally on your device, changes to our privacy practices would only apply to future versions of the software.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@ambitbudget.com.
