Feature Overview

Powerful tools, beautifully simple.

Smart Dashboard

Your complete financial picture at a glance — balances, spending trends, budget status, and upcoming bills.

Easy Import

Paste from Excel or upload CSV files. Ambit auto-maps columns and handles different bank formats.

🏷️

Auto-Categorize

Create keyword rules once. Every future import gets categorized instantly.

Budget Tracking

Set monthly budgets per category. See what's on track and where you can save.

📄

Bill Management

Track due dates, billing frequency, and payment status. Never miss a bill again.

📈

Balance Forecast Plus & Pro

See where your balance is headed — end of month and next month projections based on your bills and income.

📋

Monthly Review Plus & Pro

Start each month by reviewing what you budgeted vs. what you spent. Adjust budgets in one click.

📆

Calendar Year View Plus & Pro

A 12-month spending grid by category with budget color-coding. Click any cell to drill down into individual transactions.

🏦

Per-Account Budgets Plus & Pro

Split budgets across checking accounts. Set different grocery budgets for each account, or keep one global default.

🔄

Payment Scheduling Pro

Set payment frequencies for income and bills — biweekly, semi-monthly, quarterly, or annual. The forecast knows exactly when money moves.

📅

Day-by-Day Projections Pro

A daily timeline of every bill, income, and budget event — showing your running balance through next month.

💳

Debt Planner Pro

Snowball or avalanche strategies with custom payment plans, scenario modeling, and month-by-month progress tracking.

💰

Lump Sum Analyzer Pro

Got a bonus or tax refund? See the optimal way to deploy it across your debts with side-by-side strategy comparisons and interest savings.
